Who Are We?

MEADOWS TOUCH SUPPORT FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization committed to supporting underprivileged populations by addressing critical healthcare, social, and environmental needs. Through a combination of healthcare services, social development projects, and support for physically challenged individuals, we work tirelessly to uplift the less privileged and promote sustainable growth in underserved communities. Our foundation is led by dedicated trustees who envision a world where essential services reach everyone, regardless of their background or socioeconomic status.

Our Mission

1. Healthcare Support: Providing healthcare services to the less privileged.

2. Support for Physically Challenged Individuals: Equipping and empowering those with physical challenges.

3. Environmental and Social Development: Promoting environmental awareness and social welfare in underprivileged communities.

Success Stories

Our initiatives are making a real difference in the lives of underserved communities. Here are some of our impactful success stories:

1. Healthcare for Umuovo Community

Recently, MEADOWS TOUCH SUPPORT FOUNDATION organized a health outreach program in the Umuovo community. We provided free medical check-ups, basic treatments, and health education, benefiting over 300 people who would have otherwise struggled to access healthcare. The event brought relief and support to the residents and highlighted the impact of accessible healthcare in underserved regions.
